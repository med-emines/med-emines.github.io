About Me

Hello! I'm Mohammed El Baraka, an Industrial Management engineering student at the prestigious Mohammed VI Polytechnic University (UM6P). My academic journey, beginning with an intensive program in Mathematics and Physics, has instilled in me a strong analytical and problem-solving mindset.

I am driven by the challenge of solving complex problems, particularly through the application of Data Science, Machine Learning, and Optimization algorithms. My project work reflects my ability to bridge theoretical knowledge with practical application, from modeling oxygen diffusion in biomedical engineering to optimizing agricultural production with linear programming.

Fluent in Arabic, French, and English, I am a strong communicator and leader, actively involved in numerous university clubs and sports teams. I am always eager to learn and apply my skills to impactful, real-world scenarios.
